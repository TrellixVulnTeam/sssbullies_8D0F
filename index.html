    What does the SSS in SSSerpents & Bullies stand for you ask? It stands for Sam and Sons Serpents & Bullies. Our bullies and reptiles are raised by myself and my three sons along with my manager/wife, of course. We specialize in the American Bully breed and French Bulldogs. We are a family owned and operated kennel and serpentarium. We take pride in what we do and the purpose we aim to fulfill which is health, temperament, function (show, breeding, companion), and the overall betterment of our bully and reptile community. From our home to yours, we welcome you to our pack!
