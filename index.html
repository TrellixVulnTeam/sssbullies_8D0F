    What does the SSS in SSSerpents & Bullies stand for you ask? It stands for Sam and Sons Serpents & Bullies. Our bullies and reptiles are raised by myself and my three sons along with my manager/wife, of course. We specialize in the American Bully breed and French Bulldogs. We are a family owned and operated kennel and serpentarium.
